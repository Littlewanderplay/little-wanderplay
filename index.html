About Us

Little WanderPlay is a mobile soft play rental business based in Katy, TX. We deliver safe, modern, and engaging soft play zones to events — birthdays, family gatherings, community functions and more. Built with care and creativity, our setups are designed to bring joy to little ones and peace of mind to parents.

Our Services

  • Soft Play Equipment Rentals (ages 6 months – 4 years)
  • Custom Themes and Colors
  • Full Delivery, Setup, and Breakdown
  • Weekend and Weekday Bookings (coming soon!)
